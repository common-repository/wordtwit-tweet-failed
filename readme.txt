Provides a deatiled reason as to why your scheduled and non-scheduled WordTwit tweets may have failed.
== Description ==
This plugin was created out of a necessity for a client of mine.  Constantly we were getting tweet failed, and had no reason why.
So, in the interest of information, this plugin was born.  To view your tweet status just go to your Wordtwit -> Tweet Log then click
the errors.  You'll see a little bit more information now.

== Installation ==

= Easy Method =
1. Download the zip file.
1. Login to your `Dashboard`
1. Open your plugins bar and click `Add New`
1. Click the `upload tab`
1. Choose `content-warning-v2` from your downloads folder
1. Click `Install Now`
1. All done, now just activate the plugin

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

This plugin requires that you use WordTwit or WordTwit pro, without it this plugin is useless.
